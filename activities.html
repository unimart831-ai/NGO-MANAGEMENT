What is this system?
A centralized web application accessible via computer or phone, designed to manage daily operations, track donors, coordinate volunteers, and monitor beneficiary impact in real-time.
1. Detailed System Modules

1. Donor Management

  • Store donor names, contacts, and history
  • Categorize (Individual, Corporate, Church)
  • Track communication preferences & VIP status
Why: Never lose track of supporters; pull up a full history instantly when they call.

2. Donation Tracking

  • Record M-PESA, Cash, Bank, and In-kind
  • Link donations to specific campaigns
  • Generate receipts & track recurring donors
Why: Every shilling is accounted for; audit-ready reporting.

3. Campaign Management

  • Create fundraising drives with targets
  • Track progress % (e.g. 60% of goal raised)
  • Status tracking: Active, Completed, Pending
Why: Manage multiple drives simultaneously and identify which need focus.

4. Volunteer Management

  • Register skills, availability, and hours
  • Track activity participation
  • Emergency contact storage
Why: Know exactly who is available and how much they have contributed.

5. Beneficiary Management

  • Store personal details and program enrollment
  • Record services provided & case outcomes
  • Secure/Confidential data maintenance
Why: Accurate reporting to donors/govt on exactly who was helped.

6. Activities & Events

  • Schedule workshops, outreaches, and meetings
  • Assign staff/volunteers & set budgets
  • Calendar view for upcoming events
Why: Eliminate double-booking and maintain clear responsibility.

7. Reports & Analytics

  • Summaries (Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Yearly)
  • Visual charts & graphs
  • Export to Excel/PDF instantly
Why: Instant answers for board members and donors.

8. Role-Based Permissions

  • Admin, Manager, and Volunteer roles
  • Protect sensitive financial/personal data
  • Device-responsive (Mobile/Desktop/Tablet)
Why: Ensure security; people only see what they need for their job.
